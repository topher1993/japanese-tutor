Use the app for one short N5 workplace survival study session.

Focus on:

- Is the app understandable?
- Is the Japanese useful for work?
- Is the text readable on your phone?
- Is anything confusing, broken, or awkward?

## What To Report Immediately

Report immediately if you see:

- app crash
- blank screen
- cannot open app
- navigation blocked
- text completely cut off
- quiz cannot be completed
- feedback cannot be saved

## What Can Be Minor Feedback

These are useful but not emergency blockers:

- spacing feels slightly off
- text could be prettier
- color or button polish
- a phrase feels awkward but understandable
- you want more examples

## How To Send Feedback

Use the in-app local feedback screen:

1. Open Stats / Progress.
2. Tap Open beta feedback.
3. Choose the screen.
4. Pick rating 1-5.
5. Add a short note if useful.
6. Tap Save local feedback.

Important:

```text
Feedback is local-only. It does not automatically upload.
```

Also send Chris/Belion screenshots or notes manually for important issues.
